Our Mission

Educational upliftment

Working for the betterment of Bihar so that our children can get proper education in his own state and people will start loving their own culture and language.

linguistic upliftment

Bhojpuri is our pride, and we are taking the initiative towards strengthening the language which is on the path of loosing it's identity.

Cultural pride

We held various dialogues and discussion to help and support our society to move forward in the direction of sustainable development and awareness.


Revolution By People:

upliftment of Bihar and India

In the light of lockdown due to the Corona Virus pandemic and upcoming Bihar Assembly Elections, we came together to actively provide assistance to general public at large with the help of digital platforms and various volunteers sitting in their house. We are excited and deeply motivated to now work for the cause at whatever possible cost. Please feel free to get in touch with us, if you have any ideas or feedback regarding our plans and performance or if you have similar deep rooted feelings to serve the society, get in touch with us.
